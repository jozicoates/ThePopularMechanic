ABOUT US

Jay and Jackie, the owners of The Popular Mechanic, have over 40 years experience in import automotive repair. Jay has 22 years of professional racing experience. Formerly, he was the owner of "Best Damn Garage Around" in Berkeley Heights, and now "The Popular Mechanic."


Jay is an exceptional, meticulous mechanic, who takes the time to demonstrate solutions to keep your Porsche running its best. He provides detailed explanations, options, guidance, and estimates so you maintain an active part in maintaining your Porsche.


Jay has been trained by nationally renowned Porsche experts and is a member of multiple national Porsche tech consortiums.


Jackie manages diagnostic systems and shop communications.


Murphy and Hudson, the shop dogs, sometimes hang around during the cooler months.
